Privacy Policy

This app, Binky Bunny, does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. No information is gathered about you, your device, or your usage of the app.

1. No Data Collection

The app does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • IP addresses
  • Analytics or usage data

2. No Advertising

The app does not use Unity Ads, AdMob, or any other advertising services. No ad-related identifiers or tracking technologies are used.

3. No Third-Party SDKs

The app does not include any third-party libraries or SDKs that collect user information.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is safe for all ages. Since no data is collected, no information from children is ever stored, transmitted, or shared.

5. Changes to This Policy

If the app is updated in the future to include features that require data collection or third-party services, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those changes take effect.
